Demo: Aimpad

Aimpadâ„¢ patented technology adds full analog movement control to the traditional WASD keyboard layout. With an Aimpad keyboard you can do things not possible with a normal boring keyboard. The driving force behind Aimpad, Lance Madsen, will be on hand to demonstrate the technology he's created and licensed to Cooler Master. Come check out this innovative keyboard technology from right out of Noblesville, Indiana!

We measure how far down a mechanical key is pressed using an infrared LED. The light emitted from the LED is reflected off part of the key and then an infrared sensor measures how much light is reflected. If a low amount of light is reflected we know that the key is at its highest point. If a higher amount of light is reflected, we know that the key is moving closer to the sensor.

Learn more at Aimpad.com!

Demo: TurboKeys

Considering getting into the DIY keyboard scene? Want the spoils of that scene without doing the work yourself? TurboMech, /r/mk community contributor, will be driving up from his home in Kentucky to demonstrate building a keyboard for us. He also repairs and builds keyboards as a service, so talk to him about your dream board!

TurboKeys was founded in part with the owner's love of mechanical keyboards and seeing numerous help posts on reddit about "Can I fix this soldering?" We aim to fix that, let us assemble, repair, or modify your custom mechanical keyboard so your don't have to worry!

Whether it's a simple switch swap, LED install, QMK programming, switch harvesting, desoldering, or assemble; TurboKeys does it all! More services are being added: we are currently working to bring ball bearing homing key modifications to our services.

Learn more at TurboKeys.us!
