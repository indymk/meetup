Indy Mechanical Keyboard Meetup

May 5th, 2018 in Downtown Indianapolis from 2-6PM

Please RSVP »

What

Whether you're brand new to this hobby, or you've been building your own boards for years, come join us to chat about (and gawk at) mechanical keyboards and all the accessories for an afternoon. We'll be in beautiful downtown Indianapolis, only a few blocks from Lucas Oil Stadium, Banker's Life Fieldhouse, and Monument Circle in one of the area's premier co-working facilities, Launch Indy, within tech venue Union 525. Also, there will be keyboards. Lots of 'em.'
